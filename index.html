Premise · The Story Engine

AI can generate motion. It cannot generate meaning.

Every ASTER film is built around a five-beat story engine — a deliberate sequence of human tension, pressure, product relevance, proof, and desire. The image is directed, not generated.

B.01

Human truth

An honest emotion the viewer recognises before any brand appears.

B.02

Pressure

A tension that demands resolution, not decoration.

B.03

Product relevance

The product earns its place by answering the pressure.

B.04

Proof

A moment of visible, sensory evidence — not a claim.

B.05

Desire

The viewer leaves wanting the transformation, not the object.

P.01 · Tension

Begin with what the viewer feels.

The frame opens on a human moment before the brand has earned the right to speak.

P.02 · Relevance

The product enters only when needed.

No staged glamour. The product arrives as the answer the story has been asking for.

P.03 · Desire

Close on transformation, not transaction.

The last image is the one the viewer carries. We engineer it on purpose.

FAQ · Before the First Call

Before the first call.

Practical answers to the questions most often raised before an engagement begins.

Q.01How do the rates compare to studios and freelancers?
ASTER is structured between traditional studio production and freelance execution. The value is not only asset generation; it is strategy, story, visual DNA, motion control, finishing direction, and platform-ready delivery under one system.
Q.02Why quote regulated industries separately?
Legal, medical, financial, beauty-claim, alcohol, automotive, and other regulated categories may require additional claim review, disclaimers, compliance checks, and approval cycles.
Q.03What does the first conversation look like?
The first conversation identifies the product, category, viewer tension, commercial objective, desired emotional outcome, usage platforms, and production constraints.
Q.04Who owns the final files?
Final ownership and usage rights are defined per engagement. Licensed music, voice, likenesses, AI-generated assets, stock elements, and third-party materials are reviewed before delivery.
Q.05How many engagements per quarter?
ASTER is intentionally selective. Capacity is limited to preserve control, quality, and direct authorship.
Q.06Do you use AI-generated assets in final delivery?
Yes, when commercially usable and properly reviewed. AI may generate assets, but the final master is supervised through edit, grade, sound, language, legal, and platform QC.
Q.07How is product accuracy protected?
Product references, hero plates, labels, geometry, color, and approved key frames are locked before motion production. Product deformation is corrected or composited when needed.
Q.08What platforms are delivered?
Depending on scope: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1, 4:5, 15s, 30s, 9s, 6s, CTV, OLV, Reels, TikTok, Shorts, pre-roll, and display derivatives.
